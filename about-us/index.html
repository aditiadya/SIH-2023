About Us

Wherever food is needed,
we can grow it.

We’re not afraid of big, delicious plans. At Bowery, we’re helping secure the future of food by growing smart produce that’s more reliable and less harmful—wherever it’s needed.

If we want to secure tomorrow, we need a smarter way. Starting today.
At Bowery

We’re creating alongside nature—not harvesting from it.

Powered by BoweryOS, our proprietary operating system, our network of indoor farms are growing food smarter for more people in more places.

  • 02 Bountiful, reliable yields

    Our tech takes flavor back to its roots. Not only is our produce grown to be true to its original taste, we’re even developing proprietary varieties that expand culinary possibilities.
